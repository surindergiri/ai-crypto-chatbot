# A Brief History of Bitcoin

## Origins
Bitcoin was invented in 2008 by an unknown person or group of people using the name Satoshi Nakamoto. The currency began use in 2009 when its implementation was released as open-source software.

## The Genesis Block
The first block of the chain, known as the Genesis Block, was mined by Nakamoto on January 3, 2009. Embedded in the coinbase of this block was the text: "The Times 03/Jan/2009 Chancellor on brink of second bailout for banks."

## Pizza Day
On May 22, 2010, Laszlo Hanyecz made the first commercial transaction using Bitcoin, buying two Papa John's pizzas for 10,000 BTC.

## Market Cycles
- **2013**: Bitcoin reached $1,000 for the first time.
- **2017**: The bull run took it to nearly $20,000.
- **2021**: Bitcoin hit an all-time high of nearly $69,000.
